Digital Democracy

The 2026 shift to e-governance in Kathmandu.

The 2026 general election in Nepal marks a turning point. With the introduction of blockchain-verified voter registration pilot programs in Bagmati Province, the country is stepping into a new era of digital democracy.

Challenges remain regarding rural connectivity, but the youth demographic's demand for transparency is driving the Election Commission to adopt open-source protocols for result aggregation.
