Youth Surge

Gen Z is no longer just watching from the sidelines.

Data indicates that voters under 35 now constitute 45% of the electorate. This demographic shift is forcing traditional parties to pivot from legacy manifestos to tech-centric platforms focusing on job creation in the digital economy.

Social media campaigns have replaced door-to-door canvassing as the primary engagement tool in urban centers like Pokhara and Kathmandu.
