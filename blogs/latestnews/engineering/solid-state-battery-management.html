Powering the EVs of 2026.

Solid-state batteries have finally hit mass production. However, their unique thermal and charging characteristics require entirely new Battery Management Systems (BMS).

Algorithmic Charging

We are deploying adaptive charging algorithms written in C++ and Python that adjust current flow based on micro-structure analysis of the solid electrolyte. This extends battery life to over 500,000 miles.
