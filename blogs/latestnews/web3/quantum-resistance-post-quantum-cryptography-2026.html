Quantum Threat

Is your Bitcoin safe from a Q-Day attack?

With 5,000-qubit computers now online, legacy encryption (RSA/ECC) is at risk. Ethereum's "Pectra" upgrade has introduced quantum-resistant signature schemes (STARKs) to safeguard user funds.

The race is on to migrate the entire internet's security layer before a hostile actor achieves decryption supremacy.
