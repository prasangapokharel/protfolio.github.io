DePIN Revolution

Real-world infrastructure, powered by tokens.

In 2026, DePIN (Decentralized Physical Infrastructure Networks) has overtaken DeFi in total value locked. From decentralized WiFi hotspots to community-owned solar grids, token incentives are building physical networks faster than centralized telcos ever could.

Projects incentivizing users to share GPU compute for AI training are currently offering the highest yields in the market.
