AI & The 2024 Election: Democracy's New Frontier

February 1, 2024 • 9 min read • Technology & Politics

The First "AI Election"

The 2024 election cycle is being dubbed the first true "AI Election." Generative AI tools have lowered the barrier to entry for creating hyper-realistic content, both legitimate and malicious. From automated phone banking to personalized ad generation, AI is revolutionizing campaign operations.

The Deepfake Threat

The most pressing concern is the proliferation of deepfakes—AI-generated audio and video that impersonates candidates. We have already seen instances of "robocalls" using cloned voices to discourage voting. This technology threatens to erode the shared reality essential for democratic discourse.

Micro-Targeting on Steroids

Beyond fake content, AI allows for unprecedented micro-targeting. Campaigns can now generate thousands of variations of a message, tailored to the specific psychological profile of individual voters. While effective for mobilization, this raises serious privacy and manipulation concerns.
