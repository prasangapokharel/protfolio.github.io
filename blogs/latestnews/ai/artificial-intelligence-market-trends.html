Artificial Intelligence Market Trends: Beyond the Hype

February 1, 2024 • 8 min read • Tech Industry

The Implementation Phase

After the explosive "discovery" phase of 2023, the AI market is shifting towards implementation. Companies are moving from experimental pilots to full-scale production deployments. This shift is driving demand for "AI Infrastructure" — the chips, cloud services, and data centers needed to run these models.

Vertical AI

While general-purpose models like GPT-4 grab headlines, the real value for many enterprises lies in "Vertical AI" — models trained on specific industry data.

  • Healthcare: Drug discovery and personalized treatment plans.
  • Finance: Fraud detection and algorithmic trading.
  • Legal: Contract analysis and case research.

Small Language Models (SLMs)

Efficiency is the new frontier. Developers are focusing on creating smaller, more efficient models that can run on edge devices (like smartphones) without needing constant cloud connectivity. This trend promises to make AI more private, faster, and cheaper to run.
