Vertical Harvest

Agriculture without soil, sun, or pesticides.

Urban vertical farms are now supplying 40% of fresh produce in major cities. The secret is the IoT automation layer managed by Python microservices.

Every photon of light and drop of nutrient solution is calculated by AI to maximize growth rates. These systems are self-correcting, adjusting humidity and temperature in real-time to create the perfect growing season, every day of the year.
