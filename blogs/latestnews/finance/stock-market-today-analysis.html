Stock Market Today: Volatility Returns as Tech Sector Corrects

February 1, 2024 • 5 min read • Market News

Market Overview

Stocks finished lower today as investors digested the latest earnings reports from major tech giants. The S&P 500 dipped 0.8%, while the tech-heavy NASDAQ led the decline, dropping 1.2%. The Dow Jones Industrial Average remained relatively flat, supported by strength in the energy and healthcare sectors.

Key Drivers

  • Earnings Season: Mixed results from "Magnificent Seven" stocks have cooled the AI-driven rally.
  • Federal Reserve Comments: Hawkish remarks from Fed officials suggest rate cuts may be delayed further into the year.
  • Bond Yields: The 10-year Treasury yield ticked up, putting pressure on growth valuations.

Sector Watch

Technology: Underperforming as profit-taking sets in after months of gains.
Energy: Outperforming as oil prices rebound on supply concerns.
Consumer Staples: Showing resilience as investors seek defensive plays.
