Crypto Market Update 2025: Maturation & Regulation

February 1, 2024 • 7 min read • Crypto & Web3

The New Normal

The cryptocurrency market in 2025 looks vastly different from the wild west of the previous decade. With the approval of spot ETFs and clearer regulatory frameworks in major jurisdictions, crypto has firmly entered its institutional phase.

Bitcoin's Post-Halving Cycle

Historically, the year following a halving event sees significant price appreciation. 2025 is proving to be no exception, though with lower volatility than previous cycles. Institutional flows are dampening the extreme swings, making Bitcoin a more attractive asset for corporate treasuries.

DeFi and Real World Assets (RWA)

The tokenization of Real World Assets is the defining trend of 2025. From real estate to treasury bills, traditional assets are moving on-chain, unlocking liquidity and creating new opportunities for DeFi protocols.
